Average

Average can be defined as the sum of all the numbers divided by the total number of values

Mean

Mean is the average of the given numbers and is calculated by dividing the sum of given numbers by the total number of numbers.

Variance

Variance is the expected value of the squared variation of a random variable from its mean value, in probability and statistics.

Standard deviation

Standard deviation formula is used to find the values of a particular data that is dispersed.
